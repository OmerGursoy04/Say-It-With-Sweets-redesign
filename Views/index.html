Welcome to Say It With Sweets, your friendly neighborhood spot for all things sweet! We're all about making your celebrations extra special with our yummy cupcakes, cookies, cakes, and more. Whether you're cheering for Penn State with our special themed treats or getting into the holiday spirit with our Christmas goodies, we've got something for everyone. We take pride in using really good stuff to make our treats. This means fresh ingredients and lots of care go into everything we bake, making sure you get the tastiest and prettiest treats around.
Our bakers love to mix the old ways of baking with fun, new ideas, so every bite is a little surprise of deliciousness. At Say It With Sweets, we're not just about baking; we're about creating happy memories. Whether you're looking for a birthday cake, something fun for game day, or festive treats for the holidays, we're here to make it happen. Come on by and let us add a sprinkle of joy to your day with our sweet treats!
